DOG BODY LANGUAGE DECODER:
1. Tail Wagging to Right side: Positive emotion, happy, excited.
2. Tail Wagging to Left side: Negative emotion, anxious, alert.
3. Yawning: If not tired, this indicates STRESS or discomfort (an appeasement signal).
4. "Whale Eye" (showing whites of eyes): Fear, anxiety, guarding behavior.
5. Play Bow (front down, butt up): "Let's play!", friendly, checking safety.
6. Lip Licking: Nervousness or appeasement (saying "I am not a threat").
7. Exposed Belly: Submission or extreme trust/comfort.
8. Raised Hackles (hair on back): Arousal, fear, or aggression.

CAT BODY LANGUAGE DECODER:
1. Slow Blink: "I love you", trust, contentment.
2. Tail Straight Up: Happy, confident, friendly greeting.
3. Tail Thumping/Swishing: Irritated, getting angry, over-stimulated.
4. Ears Turned Back ("Airplane Mode"): Annoyed, fearful, ready to fight.
5. Kneading (making biscuits): Comfort, remembering kittenhood.
